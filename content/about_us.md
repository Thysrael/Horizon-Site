# About Us

## What is Horizon?

Horizon is an AI-powered news aggregation platform designed for developers, researchers, and tech enthusiasts who want to stay informed without the noise.

## Why Horizon Exists?

AI is great at filtering and summarizing, but the **human touch** is absolutely irreplaceable.
Everyone has a unique taste for news.
Making sense of a complex story often requires reading human comments and debates.
Most importantly, discovering those "hidden gem" news sources relies entirely on human curation and sharing.
Horizon is designed to augment this human element, not replace it.
