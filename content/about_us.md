# About Us

## What is Horizon?

Horizon is an AI-powered news aggregation platform designed for developers, researchers, and tech enthusiasts who want to stay informed without the noise.

## Our Mission

**Cut through the noise. Surface what matters.**

We believe in:
- **Quality over quantity** - AI scoring filters out low-value content
- **Developer-first** - Built by developers, for developers
- **Open source** - Transparent, community-driven development
- **Privacy focused** - Your data stays local

## The Team

Horizon is maintained by a small team of developers passionate about information curation:

- **Core Maintainers**: Active contributors from the open source community
- **Contributors**: 50+ developers worldwide
- **Users**: Trusted by 10,000+ developers daily

## Technology Stack

- **Python** - Core data processing
- **Next.js** - Web interface
- **PostgreSQL** - Data storage
- **OpenAI/Anthropic** - AI scoring
- **Docker** - Easy deployment
